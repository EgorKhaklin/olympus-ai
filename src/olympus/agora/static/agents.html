How agents inhabit the substrate

Every LLM call is made as a Greek role: hephaestus, momus, cassandra, athena, figure_proposer. The role determines the system prompt (figure docstring + the constitution S1–S8 + the AP catalog) and the output schema.

The model thinks in the mythology; the substrate enforces the constitution on the output. Pan still gates ratification; Momus still contests; Zeus still ratifies.
